Real Mirror

photo: Verena Tscherner

music: Patrick K.-H.

WHAT if the medium fails to disappear by using a non-normative way of generating perception, by taking advantage of the error/glitch derived by the medium itself?

Real Mirror is an interactive object that transforms the viewer's self-image into new, spatial and physical forms of perception. The constructed virtual space is based on a VR system that is accessible through smart glasses. The interface between body and virtual space - a navigation sphere - provides a virtual 360° view of the environs and reacts on spectator’s movements of the sphere. In these rooms the user can navigate and lose him/herself at the same time. An interplay between two influencing dynamics: the changing environment, triggered by one's own movement in space.
